Pollution Impact
Urban areas generate a large amount of plastic waste, and much of it ends up in rivers.
Single-use plastics like bottles, bags, and packaging are commonly discarded irresponsibly, finding their way into waterways.
The presence of plastic debris in rivers not only harms aquatic species but can also contribute to the breakdown of plastic into microplastics, which have widespread ecological impacts.
